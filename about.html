About Me

This curiosity I feel lends itself to the computing sector and especially software design and consultancy. It's amazing how someone can approach you with a problem and a couple of months later a finished product is delivered at the end. Other Interests

My other Major interests more or less all revolve around some sort of car. I'm a car enthusiast and I'm a big fan of anything to do with Audi, McLaren, Aston Martin, F1, and the WEC. If it has an engine and it is quick, then I'm interested in it.
